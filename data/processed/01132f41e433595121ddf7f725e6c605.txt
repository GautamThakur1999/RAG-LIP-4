ICICI Prudential ELSS Tax Saver Fund Overview

Fund Managers: Harish Bihani, Sharmila D'mello
Scheme Category: ELSS (Equity Linked Savings Scheme)
Inception Date: 19-Aug-1999

Investment Objective:
To generate long-term capital appreciation by investing predominantly in equity and equity related instruments, while also offering tax benefits under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act, 1961.

Key Facts:
- Minimum SIP amount is ₹500.
- Minimum lump sum investment is ₹500.
- The scheme has a mandatory statutory lock-in period of 3 years from the date of allotment.
- Because of the 3-year lock-in period, there is a Nil exit load. Investors cannot redeem their units before the completion of 3 years.
- The expense ratio for the Direct Plan is approximately 1.09% and for the Regular Plan is 1.78%.
- Benchmark: Nifty 500 TRI.
- Risk-o-meter: Very High Risk.
- Tax Benefit: Investments up to ₹1.5 Lakhs in a financial year qualify for deduction under Section 80C.

Asset Allocation:
- Equity and Equity related instruments: 80% to 100%
- Debt & Money Market Instruments: 0% to 20%
